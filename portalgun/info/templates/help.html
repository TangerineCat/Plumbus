Your Journey Begins One Dimmension at a Time

There are tons of universes for you to visit. However you will need to enter the coordinates for the Portal gun to tell you how to get to the location. To do this, click PORTAL in the menu

Throughout your adventures, you will find new dimmensions to go to. Simply enter the dimmension name into the portal gun and press go! You cannot travel to a dimmension that you don't know yet. Make sure to read the instructions of each activity carefully

To review the dimmensions you've unlocked, go to SCHEDULE in the menu.
